Introduction

Cryptography is the science of securing information by transforming it into a form that cannot be easily understood by unauthorized users. In this project, we explore two fundamental techniques in the field of cryptography: the Caesar Cipher and the RSA algorithm.

The Caesar Cipher is one of the oldest encryption methods, based on letter shifting. The RSA algorithm, on the other hand, is a powerful modern technique that uses mathematical concepts such as prime numbers and modular arithmetic.

This web application provides detailed explanations of both techniques, followed by interactive tools where users can test encryption and decryption processes in real time.
